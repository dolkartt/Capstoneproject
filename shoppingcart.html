Returns and Exchange Policy

At The New Modern, we take pride in providing high-quality products to our customers. Due to the nature of our business, we offer all items as a final sale. This means that once an item has been purchased, it cannot be returned or exchanged. We understand that sometimes there may be issues with your order, such as a damaged or defective item. In such cases, we will make every effort to resolve the issue to your satisfaction. Please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order if you have any concerns.
